About the Survey System

Survey System is a lightweight platform to create, publish and collect responses from surveys. It's built for fast setup and easy sharing.

Key Features

  • Create surveys with multiple question types.
  • Publish surveys via link or survey code for quick sharing.
  • Collect responses and view basic analytics.
  • Manage your profile, view your surveys and results from the dashboard.
